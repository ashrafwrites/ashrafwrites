## 🖋️ About Me

I'm **Ashraf Mansha** — an SEO Content Strategist and copywriter helping **SaaS, AI, e-commerce, and agency brands** across the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and Europe turn search traffic into real customers.

I write content that ranks *and* reads like a human wrote it — no robotic keyword-stuffing, no generic filler. Just clear, strategic, story-driven copy built to convert.

- 🔭 Currently building my personal brand & portfolio site
- ✍️ Specializing in SEO blog content, landing pages, and brand copywriting
- 🎯 Focused on value-based pricing and long-term client retainers
- 🌍 Working with clients across English-speaking Western markets
- 💬 Ask me about content strategy, on-page SEO, or conversion copy
